### 1\. Keyword Strategy (Right Intent)

**What we do:**

- Audit existing keywords (identify low-intent, high-cost waste)  
- Research high-intent keywords specific to pet care:  
  - For vets: "emergency vet \[city\]", "vet appointment \[area\]", "\[pet condition\] vet", "after-hours vet"  
  - For daycares: "dog daycare \[city\]", "dog boarding \[dates\]", "puppy socialization near me"  
- Build keyword tiers: high-intent (expensive, converts well), mid-intent (good volume), low-intent (eliminate)  
- Ongoing optimization: kill low-converting keywords, scale high-converters

**Result:** Higher conversion rate. Lower cost per lead. Better ROI.

---

**\[Block 2 — background \#F3F4F6, padding 24px, border-radius 8px\]**

### 2\. Landing Page Alignment (Right Message)

**What we do:**

- Audit your current landing pages (if any)  
- If needed, build conversion-optimized landing pages:  
  - For vets: specific landing page per service (emergency, routine checkup, surgery, etc.)  
  - For daycares: specific pages for daycare vs. boarding vs. trial day  
- Clear CTAs ("Book Now", "Call for Appointment", "Schedule Tour")  
- Mobile-optimized (60% of clicks are mobile)  
- Fast loading (Google penalizes slow pages)  
- Social proof (reviews, trust signals visible)

**Result:** 2–3x higher conversion from ad click to lead/booking.

- Setup conversion tracking:

  - Lead quality scoring  
- Daily dashboard showing:  
  - Cost per lead (CPL)  
  - Cost per appointment (CPA)  
  - Click-through rate (CTR)  
  - Conversion rate  
  - Return on ad spend (ROAS)

**Result:** You know exactly what each lead costs. You can make smarter investment decisions.

- Always: testing new audiences, bid strategies, match types

**Measurement:** CPL trending down 5–10% monthly. Conversion rate increasing. ROAS improving.

**Result:** Month 1 \= baseline. Month 2–3 \= 20–30% efficiency improvement. Month 4+ \= 40–50% better ROI than baseline.

## SECTION 6: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (AEO)

**\[H2\]** Questions Pet Care Owners Ask About Google Ads

---

**Q: Why should we use Google Ads instead of organic Google ranking?** A: Organic takes 6+ months. Ads start working in weeks. Both are needed, but Ads gets you immediate visibility while you build organic ranking.

**Q: How much should we spend on Google Ads?** A: 2–5% of your target monthly revenue is standard. For vets: $500–1,500/month typical. For daycares: $300–1,200/month typical. We'll recommend based on your situation.

**Q: Does Google Ads work in rural areas?** A: Yes, but with lower search volume. We adjust strategy (keyword combinations, bid strategies) for lower-volume markets. ROI is still strong.

**Q: Can we test Ads with a small budget?** A: Yes. $300–500/month is enough to test. We recommend 2–3 months to optimize before scaling.

**Q: What's the difference between Search Ads and Performance Max?** A: Search \= text ads in search results (high intent). Performance Max \= Google's AI optimizing across all channels. We use both, but Search is usually priority for pet care.

**Q: Do we need a website for Google Ads to work?** A: Ideally yes. You need a landing page to drive conversions. If you don't have one, we can build a simple conversion page as part of setup.

**Q: How do we know the leads are real?** A: Conversion tracking. We set up phone call tracking (including recording), form submissions, appointment bookings. You see exactly what each lead is.

**Q: Can we pause ads anytime?** A: Yes. But we recommend staying with it for 2–3 months to optimize. Pausing and restarting resets learning phase.
